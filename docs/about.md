## Summary

Jordi Villa i Freixa is a full professor at the Universitat de Vic - Universitat Central de Catalunya (UVic-UCC) and leads the **Computational Biochemistry and Biophysics Lab (CBBL)**. His training includes a degree in Chemistry (UAB, 1993) and a PhD in Chemistry with honors (UAB, 1998). He has developed his academic career at UVic-UCC, Universitat Pompeu Fabra, and Universitat Internacional de Catalunya, and has held university management roles such as vice-rector and school director. He has also completed research stays at universities in the United States and Europe.

His track record includes participation in European and national projects, more than 60 publications in indexed journals, and supervision of PhD theses. He has served as evaluator and member of scientific and academic quality committees, and co-founded the company aScidea (Computational Biology Solutions).
